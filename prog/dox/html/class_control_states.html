float ControlStates::minimumJerk ( float  t)

Calculates minimum jerk trajectory point. Allows to avoid step response and hence obtaining smoother trajectory. The smaller T, the more rapid response. Similar to step response achieved by higher order systems.

[in]t- time in [ms]
